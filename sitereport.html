Experience of learning basics

I had wonderful experience of learning the basics of Web development, coding, and design. It was great experience with many ups and downs. Initial stage of learning was abit confusing and difficult. I felt difficult in the implementation of tags and codes at the initial stage of learning. But, consulting and discussion with module tutor and friends, helped me to tackle those difficulties. In this short period of learning, I got many lessons from those ups and downs which will help me in my upcoming days. Learning this module was great. I learned more than my expectations in very short period of time. I wish I could get more than my expection in the coming days too.

The development of this portfolio site is my first experience of webpage development.
